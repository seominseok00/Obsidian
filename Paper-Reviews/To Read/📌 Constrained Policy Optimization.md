[[🇺🇸 Model-based Safe Deep Reinforcement Learning via a Constrained Proximal Policy Optimization Algorithm]]

In more recent, a trust region based constrained policy optimization (CPO) framework is proposed, which involved approximation of the problem using surrogate functions for both the objective and the constraints and included a projection step on policy parameters that needed backtracking line search, making it complicated and time-consuming.

CPO showed near constraint satisfaction in every iteration of the policy updates in standard MuJoCo environments modified for safe exploration, but didn't yield a constraint satisfying policy in challenging Safe RL benchmark Safety Gym.
